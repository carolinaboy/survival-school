About Me

My name is Mr. Clark. I went to The Citadel and majored in biology. I went into the army for 4 years and went on 2 tours to Syria and Iraq. I am married and have 3 children. I love to hunt and fish. I am from Greenville, SC. I teach weapons building at survival schools academy.

What We Will Learn

We will learn many things this year. Such as how to build a gun that has been taken apart, how to make weapons in any climate and surrounding.

Required Materials

  • Combat Knife
  • Compass
  • Yourself
  • A gun (if you have one)
  • Boots

Information

Every week we will have a test on types of weapons and where they are from. For your final exam, you will create a historic weapon of your choice and make it.
