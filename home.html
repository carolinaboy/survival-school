Rooming

You will be rooming with a person that we put with you. No exceptions. Only way you can switch is if the person trys to hurt you. There will be a curfew of 10 o'clock. If students don't report to their rooms by this time, they will have PT.

Parent's Day Information

On Parents Day, you will be able to visit your student. You may only visit on this day or their birthday. This way, we are limiting the distractions that these students may face.

What to Expect

You can expect a military type school that teaches survival skills. We are in a high school league for athletics, but we aren't the average school. Every student will participate in an excersize class. That way they are fit and ready for the climate. We will be hard on your student so don't get worried, we will still treat them fairly.
